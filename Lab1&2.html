Blink Test 1:

This is a case 1 of Blinking test. Here we just used default High and Low conditions and we observe Red LEG blinking as it is a default LED.

Blink Test 2:

This is a case 2 of Blinking test. Here we have updated the default to BLUE LED on High and Low conditions and we observe BLUE LEG blinking for every 1 Second.

Blink Test 3:

This is a case 3 of Blinking test. Here we have updated the default to GREEN LED on High and Low conditions and we observe GREEN LEG blinking for every 1 Second.

Blink Test 4:

This is a case 4 of Blinking test. Here we have updated the default to RED, BLUE, GREEN LEDs on High and Low conditions and we observe All three colour LEDs blinking one after another for every 5 Second.
